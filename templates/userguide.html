Select Your Category and Enter Grades

Start by selecting the program category that matches your educational goals:

University bachelor's degrees

College diploma courses

Certificate Programs

Technical certificates

KMTC Programs

For Degree Programs: Enter your KCSE subject grades. Our system automatically calculates cluster points based on your subject performance.

Multi-Category Discount: First category costs KSH 199. Additional categories only KSH 99 each!

2

Provide Required Information

After selecting your category, you'll need to provide:

  • Valid Email Address: Used to send your results and for account recovery
  • KCSE Index Number: Your unique examination identification number
  • Phone Number: For payment verification and results retrieval

Important: Ensure your email address is active and entered correctly. All results and confirmation emails will be sent to this address.

3

Secure Payment via Paystack

We use Paystack for secure, reliable payments. Paystack supports multiple payment methods:

Credit/Debit Cards

Visa, Mastercard, Verve

Mobile Money

M-Pesa, Airtel Money, Tigo Pesa

Bank Transfer

Direct bank transfers

Pricing Structure

Service Amount Description
First Category Check KSH 199 Initial payment for your first category check
Each Additional Category KSH 99 Discounted rate for checking multiple categories

Payment Process:

  1. Click "Proceed to Payment" after entering your details
  2. You'll be redirected to Paystack's secure payment page
  3. Choose your preferred payment method
  4. Complete the payment process on Paystack
  5. You'll be automatically redirected back to see your results

Example: Checking Degree (KSH 199) + Diploma (KSH 99) + Certificate (KSH 99) = Total KSH 397

4

Viewing Your Results

After successful payment, you'll immediately see your personalized results showing:

  • All courses you qualify for based on your KCSE grades
  • Institutions offering each course
  • Programme codes for KUCCPS application
  • Detailed entry requirements
  • Course duration and fees (where available)

Retrieving Previous Results

If you've already made payment and need to retrieve your results:

  1. Go to the "Already Paid" section in the main menu
  2. Enter your Paystack transaction reference
  3. Provide your KCSE index number
  4. Click retrieve to view your results again

Note: Keep your Paystack transaction receipt. You can use the transaction reference to retrieve your results anytime.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pay using mobile money?

Yes! Paystack supports M-Pesa, Airtel Money, and other mobile money services. Just select "Mobile Money" on the Paystack payment page.

How do I calculate cluster points for degree programs?

You don't need to! Our system automatically calculates your cluster points based on your KCSE subject grades. Just enter your subject grades, and we'll handle the calculations.

Is Paystack secure for payments?

Absolutely! Paystack is PCI DSS compliant, which means it meets the highest security standards for payment processing. Your financial information is completely secure.

Can I check courses for multiple categories at once?

Yes! Select all the categories you're interested in during the selection process. You'll get a discounted rate for additional categories.
